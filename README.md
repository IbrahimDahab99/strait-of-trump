# Strait of Trump (Satire)

A free browser game about the 2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis.

You play as a fictional US president trying to reopen a strait that 
was open before your operation closed it.

## Play it

👉 [Play the game](https://ibrahimdahab99.github.io/strait-of-trump/)

## What is this

A satirical arcade game built with HTML, CSS and JavaScript — no 
frameworks, no install, runs in any browser.

You have mines to clear, tankers to escort, a live Wolf News ticker, 
and a cartoon Trump who reacts to everything you do with his actual quotes.

Every military action makes things worse.
The only way to win is not obvious.
Figure it out.

## Legal

This is a non-commercial work of political satire and parody.

Protected under Art. 21 of the Italian Constitution and Art. 10 of 
the European Convention on Human Rights.

Real quotes are attributed with their source.
Fictional dialogue is labeled as such.
No real financial transaction occurs in this game.

Donald Trump is a public figure. Satire of public figures on matters 
of public concern is a legally protected form of expression.
