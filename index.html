🎮 MISSION BRIEFING
HOW TO PLAY
📍 THE SITUATION
The Strait of Hormuz — a narrow waterway carrying 20% of the world's oil — is now heavily mined and blocked. You play a fictional US president. Your job: open the strait. How you do it is up to you.
Warning: this is harder than it looks. And easier than it should be. But also impossible. You'll see.
🗺️ WHAT'S ON THE MAP
💣
SEA MINES
Iranian contact mines floating in the shipping lanes. They pulse red. Click them to detonate them safely. If you ignore them, they drift and Iran deploys more. If a tanker hits one... big problem.
🚢
OIL TANKERS
Friendly tankers attempting to cross from left to right. Click them to escort them through. They travel straight — if there's a mine in their path, they'll hit it. Clear the path first.
📊 YOUR STATUS BARS
🚧 BLOCKADE
How sealed the strait is. Starts at 95%. Clear mines and escort tankers to reduce it. Iran keeps reinforcing — it will creep back up if you do nothing.
🛢️ OIL FLOW
How much oil is getting through. Starts at 5%. Every escorted tanker helps. This is your progress bar.
🔥 DANGER
The most important bar. Watch this one carefully. It starts low and climbs over time. If it hits 100%... the game ends badly. Keep it under control.
💥 YOUR OPTIONS (Left Panel)
🚢 DEPLOY NAVY
Send the 5th Fleet to patrol. Clears some blockade and opens lanes. Has a cooldown before you can use it again.
🧹 MINE SWEEP
Deploy mine-clearing vessels. Big reduction in blockade and mines. Slower cooldown but very effective.
✈️ SCRAMBLE JETS
F/A-18s intercept threats and clear the airspace. Aggressive option — clears the blockade fast.
🕊️ NEGOTIATE WITH IRAN
Open a diplomatic back-channel. Reduces blockade. Takes multiple rounds to produce results. Long cooldown.
🇪🇺 CALL EUROPE
Contact NATO allies and ask for naval support. See what they say.
💣 STRIKE IRAN
Launch a military strike. Reduces the blockade fast. High risk option with consequences. Use carefully.
📈 MANIPULATE MARKETS
Pass some market intelligence to the family. Watch the family profit chart update. Oil prices will react.
📱 TWEET AGGRESSIVELY
Post ALL CAPS on Truth Social. Fast cooldown. Markets react. Iran reacts. The world reacts. Maybe not the way you want.

💡 TIPS
→ Clear mines before tankers arrive — tankers travel in straight lines and will hit anything in their path.
→ Watch the DANGER bar — it matters more than anything else on screen.
→ Combos are your best friend — aim to click multiple things in quick succession.
→ The spawn rate accelerates as you clear more mines — things get hectic fast.
→ Not all options are equal. Some have side effects that aren't immediately obvious. Experiment.
